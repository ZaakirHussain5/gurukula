About Gurukula International School

”dedication to academic excellence our innovative approach to learning and our nurturing creativity” Gurukula International School was founded in 2005 with Montessori, Primary and higher primary system of education. It is situated near Banavara, Arsikere Tq., Hassan District> surrounded with 6 acers with large playground and beautiful garden with peaceful atmosphere. As a feature it is Affiliated to CBSE with residential and have conductive environment for learning. The system of education for tiny-tots and young children has been widely recognized as an ideal system to guide the children in their primary age and prepare for them for higher education and make them good citizen of the country. G.I.S is guided by a commitment to excel, provides an outstanding and distinctive intellectual, social and physical environment in which students can achieve their potential. A well-educated child will be an asset to the whole nation. The dedicated efforts of the management and the teacher have been giving rewarding yield. We have a track record of cent percent result right from the inception a large number of them getting through with distinctions. six batches of X std students of our G I S went out leaving behind strong foot marks. We have a vision of providing best education to those children who hail from rural back ground a real challenge to face.
