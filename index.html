A word by our Hon. Pesident

MR. GB ANAND MURTHY

Greetings and a warm welcome to Gurukula International School! Gurukula is proud to be an International CBSE school catering to students from the ages of 2 – 18 in the Primary Years , Middle Years and the higher years At Gurukula, we are dedicated to our Mission Statement – “To provide stimulating, engaging academics integrated with enhanced opportunities for technological innovation, sports and the arts.” As you visit our campus and website, you will see that our learning community is dedicated to the development of knowledgeable, caring, confident and responsible global citizens. Our purpose-built facilities are situated on a beautiful 12-acre campus located in gavimatt, and just five minutes from banavara. Expansion of our facilities include Creative Art Space for our Primary students to accommodate our student exploration in learning, as well as investigative lab and research spaces. The facilities currently operational at the school include a beautiful swimming pool, sports hall, safe playground areas and equipment, music rooms, libraries, beautiful and comfortable boarding houses, science labs. Currently, our school accommodates over 400 students providing a diverse and rich learning community which enhances the opportunities for cultural understanding, while celebrating our host culture of India. We hope that you will explore our school further and experience what makes our school unique and proven, which we affably refer to as “The gurukala way”! With best regards, gurukula Head of School
